Baklava

Description

A recipe for baklava

Ingredients

  • Phyllo dough
  • Butter
  • Chopped nuts
  • Sugar
  • Ground cinnamon
  • Honey
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Stack eight sheets of phyllo dough, brushing with butter between each layer, to create a sturdy base.
  2. Sprinkle with a mixture of chopped nuts and cinnamon
  3. Top with two shets of phyllo dough and follow with the nut mixture. Repeat until nut mixture is gone.
  4. Follow with eight more layers of phyllo, brushing butter between each layer.
  5. Cut the baklava into diamonds or squares
  6. Bake until golden and crisp
  7. Boil water and sugar until sugar is melted. Add vanilla and honey. Remove the baklava from the oven and immediately pour syrup over it. Let cool before serving.
