Baklava

Description

A recipe for egg fried rice

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp cooking oil
  • 4 eggs, beaten with a pinch of salt
  • 4 cups of cold jasmine rice
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 4 sliced green onions

Steps

  1. Beat eggs well with a pinch of salt and sliced green onions. Set aside.
  2. Heat a wok or a skillet over high heat; add 1 tbsp cooking oil and swirl to coat. Pour beaten eggs into the wok/skillet and scramble.
  3. When the eggs are cooked 3/4 way, add the remaining 2 tbsp cooking oil and cold jasmine rice.
  4. Break down the cold rice and mix it with the eggs.
  5. Add soy sauce around the edge of the wok, so it will burn slightly for the smoky flavor. Add salt to your taste and mix everything well. Reduce heat to low if necessary.
  6. Turn off the heat, stir in green onions. Garnish with sesame seeds if desired.
